There are a great many species of spiders, yet people fear most of them. Spiders are fast and creepy, synonymous with sinister things.
There is of course, precedent for this. Many spiders are venomous, sometimes even to a lethal degree, so our fear of them is often rooted an innate instinct to keep away from danger.
However, there are several species that are harmless. For example, jumping spiders!

Now, you might think that jumping spiders are even worse, because they're just regular spiders with even more mobility. But that's not entirely the case.
While jumping spiders are still wild creatures driven by instinct like any other spider, most are harmless to humans, and they are also quite intelligent for their size.
Jumping spiders have proven to be observant, with simple problem-solving skills and some innate curiosity. They've even been shown to exibit individual personalities, based on a study in 2015! Source
